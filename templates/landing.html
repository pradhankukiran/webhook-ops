WebhookOps

A single-user, self-hosted webhook operations platform for reliable ingestion, delivery, retry, and replay of third-party webhooks.

Sending a webhook

# Configure a Source in the admin first, then POST to its slug: curl -X POST https://webhook-ops.up.railway.app/in/<source-slug>/ \ -H 'Content-Type: application/json' \ -H 'X-Webhook-Id: evt_001' \ -d '{"hello": "world"}'

What it does

Provider-aware ingest
HMAC verification for Stripe, GitHub, Shopify, Slack, and generic sources.
Durable storage
Every payload persisted before delivery; deduplicated by provider event id.
Reliable delivery
Public HTTPS or private-agent destinations, with exponential-backoff retry and dead-lettering.
Replay anything
Re-queue any event from the admin or REST API; full audit log of every transition.
